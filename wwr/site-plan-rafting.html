Overview

Purpose

The idea behind all of this is to provide an unforgettable experience for each of our adventurers. Rafting is one of those activities that pushes you to the limit and takes you out of your daily routine. Our idea is not to minimize the importance of work or study, but to help people live a life full of accomplishments and goals. Why not take them to the limit with an experience that will bring a different mentality to continue with our lives in a way that is full of achievement and adventure.

Audience

Who are your target customers? Our target customers are people who work, study (school or university). What do they want? They want to perform different tasks to use the energy they recharge every week. What are their needs that aren't being met? They all have one thing in common. Daily routine, they all perform similar activities day after day, they don't realize that there are many of them just studying and working. They can have an amazing experience out of the daily routine.
